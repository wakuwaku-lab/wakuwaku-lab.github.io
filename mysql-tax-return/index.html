Journal (仕訳帳)

Primary record of all transactions.

General Ledger (総勘定元帳)

History organized by account.

Balance Sheet (貸借対照表)

Summary of assets, liabilities, and equity.

P&L Statement (損益計算書)

Summary of revenues and expenses.
